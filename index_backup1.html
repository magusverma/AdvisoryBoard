  • IIIT - Delhi

    Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) - Delhi was created as a State University by an act of Delhi Government empowering it to do research and development and grant degrees. The institute began with its first batch of 60 BTech students in 2008. This first batch graduated in 2012 - the first Convocation took place in November, 2012. In a relatively short time, it has earned a good reputation in India and abroad for being a center of quality education and research in IT.
